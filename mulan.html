Mulan's Porridge

A comforting and nutritious porridge inspired by Mulan's journey.

Ingredients:

1 cup rice
4 cups water
1/2 cup milk (optional)
1 teaspoon salt
Optional toppings: sliced green onions, chopped peanuts, fried shallots, soy sauce

Instructions:

Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
In a pot, combine the rinsed rice and water. Bring to a boil over high heat.
Reduce the heat to low and simmer, partially covered, for about 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
If using milk, add it to the pot and stir well to combine. Cook for an additional 5 minutes.
Add salt and adjust seasoning to taste.
Serve the porridge hot, and garnish with sliced green onions, chopped peanuts, fried shallots, or a drizzle of soy sauce, if desired.
