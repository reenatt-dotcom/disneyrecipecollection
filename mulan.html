Mulan's Steamed Dumplings

Delicate and flavorful steamed dumplings inspired by the Chinese cuisine in Mulan.

Ingredients:

1 cup all-purpose flour
½ cup water
1 cup cabbage, finely chopped
½ cup carrots, grated
1 cup mushrooms, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon sesame oil
1 teaspoon ginger, grated
Salt and pepper to taste
Sliced green onions (for garnish)

Instructions:

In a large bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and water to make the dumpling wrappers. Knead until the dough is smooth and elastic. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
In a separate bowl, mix together the chopped cabbage, grated carrots, minced garlic, chopped mushrooms, soy sauce, sesame oil, grated ginger, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a thin circle to make the dumpling wrappers.
Place a spoonful of the vegetable filling onto each dumpling wrapper. Fold the wrapper in half and pinch the edges to seal the dumplings.
Arrange the dumplings in a steamer basket lined with parchment paper.
Steam the dumplings over high heat for about 15-20 minutes, or until the wrappers are translucent and the filling is cooked.
Remove from heat and garnish with sliced green onions. Serve hot.
