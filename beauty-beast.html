Beauty and the Beast Recipes

Here are some delightful vegetarian recipes inspired by the movie Beauty and the Beast:

Be Our Guest Ratatouille

A flavorful and colorful vegetable dish that will make you feel like a guest at the Beast's castle.

Ingredients:

1 eggplant, sliced
1 zucchini, sliced
1 yellow squash, sliced
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
1 onion, sliced
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
2 tablespoons tomato paste
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon dried thyme
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon dried basil
Salt and pepper to taste
Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)

Instructions:

Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
In a large baking dish, spread the minced garlic and tomato paste on the bottom.
Layer the sliced vegetables over the tomato paste, alternating the colors.
Drizzle olive oil over the vegetables and season with dried thyme, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper.
Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 40 minutes.
Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly.
Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve warm.

Gray Stuff

A mysterious and delicious dessert that Lumière recommends trying.

Ingredients:

1 package (3.4 oz) instant vanilla pudding mix
1½ cups milk
1 package (8 oz) whipped topping
3 tablespoons chocolate sandwich cookie crumbs
Gummy worms (for garnish)

Instructions:

In a mixing bowl, combine the instant vanilla pudding mix and milk. Whisk until well blended and smooth.
Add the whipped topping to the pudding mixture. Fold gently until fully incorporated.
Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour to set.
Sprinkle the chocolate sandwich cookie crumbs over the top of the set mixture.
Garnish with gummy worms and serve chilled.
