Rapunzel's Recipes

Here are some delicious vegetarian recipes inspired by the movie Tangled:

Braided Bread

Ingredients:

2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt
2 ¼ teaspoons instant yeast
1 cup warm water
2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

Inspired by Rapunzel's long, braided hair, this bread is a delicious treat fit for a princess.
Combine the flour, sugar, salt, and instant yeast in a large mixing bowl.
Add warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Mix until the dough comes together.
Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
Punch down the dough and divide it into 3 equal portions.
Roll each portion into a long rope-like shape, resembling Rapunzel's long hair.
Place the ropes side by side and pinch one end together.
Braid the ropes together, imagining Rapunzel's beautiful hair, and pinch the other end to seal.
Transfer the braided bread to a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Cover the bread with a damp cloth and let it rise for another 30 minutes.
Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
Bake the bread for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown.
Remove from the oven and let it cool before serving.
