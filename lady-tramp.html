Spaghetti with Vegetarian "Meatballs"

A classic Italian dish featuring spaghetti pasta and savory vegetarian "meatballs."

Ingredients:

8 ounces spaghetti
1 cup breadcrumbs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
1/2 cup milk (plant-based milk such as almond or soy)
1/4 cup chopped parsley
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 egg substitute (such as flaxseed meal or applesauce)
1 can tomato sauce
1 can diced tomatoes
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
In a mixing bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese (if using), milk, chopped parsley, minced garlic, and egg substitute. Mix well.
Shape the mixture into small "meatballs."
In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the vegetarian "meatballs" and cook until browned on all sides.
Remove the "meatballs" from the skillet and set aside.
In the same skillet, add the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
Bring the sauce to a simmer and add the cooked spaghetti and vegetarian "meatballs." Stir gently to coat the pasta and "meatballs" with the sauce.
Simmer for a few minutes until heated through.
Serve hot, and enjoy a romantic spaghetti dinner just like Lady and the Tramp!
